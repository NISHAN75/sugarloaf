Investment Management

At Sugarloaf Wealth Management, your portfolio will be driven by a disciplined investment process offering active equity and fixed-income strategies that optimize return across complete market cycles. Our goal is to protect, grow, and steward your resources in a way that reflects your risk tolerance and aligns with your goals.

We utilize an internal Investment Committee and collaborate with a team of CFA® charter holders to identify various investment opportunities and build sound portfolios. We believe your portfolio should be allocated to thrive regardless of the market conditions through balancing the exposure necessary to meet your long-term goals and the security needed to meet your short-term obligations.
