Taylor Manry, CFP®
Principal

If someone was ever born to be a financial planner, it’s Taylor. His dad started J.D. Manry & Associates, which eventually became Sugarloaf Wealth Management. Taylor officially joined the firm in 2007, although he’s been a part of it since his childhood.

Taylor enjoys the challenge of helping clients find solutions for each of their unique circumstances, especially in the areas of retirement, investments, and estate planning.

Taylor graduated with honors from Auburn University with a degree in finance and is a Certified Financial Planner™. He is also a member of the Financial Planning Association of Georgia.

Taylor is a fifth-generation native of Gwinnett county and resides in Walton county with his wife, son, and two dogs. As a passionate Auburn fan, Taylor enjoys cheering on the Tigers every fall.
